What does the Dovahzul of Whiterun translate to?

In the language of dragons, Dovahzul, Whiterun translates to "Hill-of-the-Dragons".

What is Whiterun's allegiance?

Initially, Jarl Balgruuf claims that he has yet to decide who to join in the Civil War, making the city of Whiterun neutral, although he does claim to owe a favor to General Tullius, to whom he seems indebted. Balgruuf also suggests he owes a favor to Ulfric Stormcloak. There are however several indicators that suggest Whiterun is leaning more towards the Empire.

The Companions have their Skyrim headquarters in Whiterun. Are they werewolves?

Although a popular belief, there is no evidence of lycanthropy in the history of The Companions.

What is Gildergreen?

In the center of Whiterun stand Gildergreen, a tree born from the branches of the Eldergleam. It is revered by the followers of Kynareth, which is why Whiterun has Skyrim's only Temple of Kynareth.

Are there previous adventurers that are a part of the Whiterun guard?

Yes, they used to be adventurers, but then they took arrows to their knees.
